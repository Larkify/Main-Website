What is Nova?

My Discord bot named Nova, originally started out as a custom bot to take advantage of helpful features for a Discord Server that's since become Partnered. As time went on, I started adding features that would display lists and guides related to technology and computing. As a TechToker, I felt like something was missing from TechTok Discord servers, and it was my Bot. So with that said, I decided to make it public, but more of a Whitelist public. I only wanted my Discord Bot in servers I know would be able to contact me if needs be. This meant that only servers related to technology and computing could add my bot. So, if you are a Discord Server based around technology and fancy my Discord bot joining, then simply click here.
